Famous Groups

BTS

BTS, or Bangtan Sonyeondan, is a South Korean group formed by Big Hit Entertainment in 2013. The group consists of seven members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook, who underwent years of intensive training before their debut. Initially, BTS focused on hip-hop, but over time they explored a variety of musical styles, always delivering lyrics that address social issues, mental health, and self-discovery. The album "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life" (2015) marked the beginning of their international recognition, followed by enormous success with "Wings" (2016) and *"Love Yourself"* (2017/2018). With songs like "DNA" and "Dynamite" BTS took the world by storm, breaking records and becoming the first South Korean group to be nominated for a Grammy. Beyond music, they are known for their cultural impact, social campaigns, and positive messages. Today, they are global icons and pioneers of K-pop on an international level.

Seventeen

Seventeen is a South Korean group formed by Pledis Entertainment in 2015. The group consists of 13 members: S.Coups, Jeonghan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Woozi, DK, Mingyu, The8, Seungkwan, Vernon, Joshua, and Dino. They are known for their unique approach to music, with several members actively involved in composing, producing, and choreographing their songs, giving them a distinctive and authentic creative identity. The name "Seventeen" represents the 13 members, the 3 sub-units, and the idea of 17 elements working together as one unified team. Their debut album, "17 Carat" was an instant success. Since then, Seventeen has gained international recognition with albums like "Love & Letter" (2016) and "Teen, Age" (2017). They are celebrated for their high-energy performances and intricate choreography. With a global fanbase, Seventeen continues to expand their influence in K-pop, solidifying their status as one of the most popular groups of their generation.

TWICE

TWICE is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment in 2015, following the reality show "Sixteen," which selected the group's nine members. The members are Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. The name "TWICE" symbolizes the dual impact the group aims to deliver: once through their music and once through their charisma. They debuted with the single "Like Ooh-Ahh" which was an instant success. Since then, the group has gained immense popularity, especially with hits like "Cheer Up," "TT" "Likey" and "Fancy." TWICE is renowned for their energetic performances and captivating musical style, which blends pop, dance, and electronic music elements. Their global fanbase, called ONCE has supported the group as they became one of the biggest names in K-pop of their generation. Over the years, TWICE has remained relevant with continuous releases and international collaborations.
